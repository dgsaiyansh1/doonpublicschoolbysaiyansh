Doon Public School is amongst the top 10 best senior secondary public schools in Delhi/NCR. The school takes pride in being awarded The Best E School in Delhi/NCR by The Outlook Magazine. The catalogue of achievements is extravagant and never fails to impress. The schools comprehensive education is empowered with passion, sincerity, dedication and excellence in the academic as well as the co-scholastic aspects. The school believes in the profuse potential of each student and ensures to explore it in its entirety.

The scientific approach to teaching lends the school its global status in the field of education, while ensuring to strike a diligent balance with constant value enforcement. A holistic approach ensures the schools classification amongst The Top 10 Best Schools IN Delhi/NCR.
